Hardware

Texas Instruments

Entire BoosterPack made with TI ICs

Open Source

Hardware is open source

Single PCB Solution

All features are built on top of a single piece of PCB

Dual H-Bridge Motor Driver - DRV8833
This motor driver features dual H-bridge. Takes in 4-input PWMs and can drive 2 motors.
Sharp Infrared Proximity Sensor
Detects objects 10 to 80 cm away by outputting an analog voltage ranging from 3.1 to 0.4V to an analog pin. [Datasheet]
Five Reflectance Sensors - QRE1113
By deciphering white from black surfaces, the reflectance sensors enable cool line tracking and maze solving capabilities. [Datasheet]

Documentation

Getting Started

We, the BoosterBot team, recommend getting started by downloading the Motor Library and the demos under the Downloads section. Attach your BoosterBot under your favorite 40-pin LaunchPad then download an Energia demo to your board.

Hardware Setup

Setting up the hardware could not be easier! Take BoosterBot out of the box and attach it under a 40-pin LaunchPad. Power the boards by using your FuelTank BoosterPack or the 3xAAA battery pack.

Software Setup

Download Energia, plug in a USB cable into your computer, download the demos and Energia libraries under the Downloads section, and download a demo application to the LaunchPad.
